BLACKOUT city-wide · every 10 days

Every 10 days the failing light is tested against the rising dark. The town's total LIGHT must MEET OR EXCEED the blackout's dark number (the red number that creeps in by LIGHT), or the town is CONSUMED and the run ends.

A FAST blackout hits in 1 day instead of 2. The word turns red to warn you. First blackout is always a safe 2-day yellow. The word also grows +15% taller each blackout (cap +115%).

Only one creep at a time. Defense uses the same survivor-day math as reclaiming: needed survivor-days = FORCE - crew + 1, capped at 3 days, each day past the cap adds risk. Undefended = the tile falls next turn.
BLACKOUT vs CREEP. BLACKOUT is the whole-town clock (LIGHT vs DARKNESS, run-ending). CREEP is a local skirmish over a single tile (survivors vs FORCE, costs you ground, not the run). Both scale with time; only the blackout ends the run.
